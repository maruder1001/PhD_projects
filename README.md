Simulation codes in Go language prepared for Mumax3. Micromagnetic simulations of spin-wave scattering were the main tool in my PhD research.
Alongside the simulation codes I have also included the postprocessing codes I have prepared in Python. Originally all of the codes were written
by me with help of my supervisors and friends at Physics Department of UAM in Poznań. The versions I have included here are acutally run through 
ChatGPT and Claude which polished them and added commentary. I hope now the codes are more readable for other users and me as well.

Folders in the repository correspond to follwoing papers:
  1. 2022_APL_Mat_10_9 -> "Magnon spectrum of Bloch hopfion beyond ferromagnetic resonance" APL Materials 10 (9)
  2. 2023_PRB_108_1_014418 -> "Modal approach to modeling spin wave scattering" Physical Review B 108 (1), 014418
